⚔️ Begin Your Quest

Connect your Goodreads export (or any Google Sheet) to start your adventure.

  1. Export from Goodreads: Go to My Books → Import and Export → Export Library. Download the CSV file.
  2. Upload to Google Sheets: Open sheets.google.com, click + New, then File → Import your downloaded CSV.
  3. Go to File → Share → Publish to web. Select your sheet, choose Comma-separated values (.csv), and click Publish.
  4. Copy the published URL and paste it below.
