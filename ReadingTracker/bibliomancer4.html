⚔️ Begin Your Quest

Connect your Goodreads export to start your adventure.
  1. Export from Goodreads: Go to My Books → Import and Export → Export Library → download the CSV.
  2. Upload to Google Sheets: Open sheets.new, then File → Import → Upload your CSV. Click Import data.
  3. Go to File → Share → Publish to web. Set dropdowns to your sheet and Comma-separated values (.csv). Click Publish.
  4. Copy the URL shown and paste it below.
