ABOUT PDG

Our Mission

Our Vision

Our Values

Helping our customers realize their   business objectives through superior workforce performance. PDG is committed to providing highly effective workforce transformation through knowledge and learning. We are committed to driving real business metrics through improved performance.

We create repeatable tools and methodologies with proven effectiveness, but we recognize each client is unique and has specialized needs. In today’s information economy, we believe knowledge and learning are key competitive advantages. But learning for its own sake is not enough; knowledge must drive key business initiatives.

We value the success of our clients, measured through the impacts on meaningful business metrics. Our success has been driven by a relentless commitment to quality that stems from our core values. PDG is a client-centric organization; we take a consultative approach; we’re relationship-oriented; and we’re flexible.
