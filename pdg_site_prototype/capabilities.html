Governance and Infrastructure

Creating an effective learning environment is much more than creating great content. PDG has extensive experience in creating the structures and repeatable processes that allow learning groups to operate effectively and efficiently. PDG has helped organizations create governance plans to create learning standards, remediation, and oversight.

PDG has a wide range of diagnostics to determine the current strengths of your organization and identify gaps and opportunities for growth and improvement. Our flagship diagnostic is the Learning Capabilities Maturity Matrix™, which can be used for a comprehensive analysis of a learning organization, or a deep dive into a particular area of opportunity or challenge.

​PDG has performed a range of needs analysis and readiness assessments including:

Emerging Technology Strategy

Performance Support Strategy

The goal of learning is performance—and on-the-job performance requires effective support tools. Performance Support augments primary learning and puts critical information at employees’ fingertips at the point-of-need.

We’re out on the leading edge and the bleeding edge, evaluating and determining the effectiveness of new learning technologies. PDG can analyze your organization’s needs and readiness, and develop an implementation roadmap to integrate new technologies into your learning strategy. We can also advise and help you build the infrastructure you need to drive success.

Organizational Change Management

Most learning initiatives involve at least an element of organizational change—and many have a huge impact on how the organization operates. PDG has effective strategies for enabling organizational change and support you in driving it through the organization.

Workforce Transformation and Optimization

When conditions change, what’s worked in the past may no longer work. To be successful in a new environment new ways of doing things need to be learned, whether its sales people that need to learn how to operate in a changing regulatory landscape or managers that need to lead their people through times of change.
